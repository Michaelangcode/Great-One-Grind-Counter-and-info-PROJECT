Changelog

All notable changes to Great One Grind Counter are documented here.

Unreleased

  • Added an "About" tab (under Settings/About) with information on the tool, its origin, and an FAQ
  • Renamed "How to Use Counter" to "How to Use Tool" and expanded it with fuller Log and Analytics sections
  • Removed Max-Weight Est tracking entirely — from the Advanced counter, All Grinds Summary, trend chart, Correlation, Grind Comparison, CSV export, and the Grind Log
  • Renamed the Advanced counter's "Max-Level" button to "Trolls" and simplified its cascading logic
  • Removed on-counter hint tooltips, now unnecessary with the simplified cascading
  • Fixed the Correlation section's math to compare each kill type against the rest of the grind instead of a total that already includes it
  • Removed the Correlation section entirely — Diamonds and Max-Level kills overlap by construction, which made the underlying math unreliable even after the fix above
  • Removed separate Basic/Advanced counter styles — there's now one unified counter (Diamond / Trolls / Total Kills), and all Great One grinds (past and future) now feed into All Grinds Summary stats, trend, and comparison
