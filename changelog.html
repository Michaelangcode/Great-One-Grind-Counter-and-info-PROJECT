Changelog

All notable changes to Great One Grind Log are documented here.

1.3.0 — Quality of Life & Polish

  • Counter numbers (Diamond, Trolls, Total Kills, Rare Fur) can now be clicked and typed directly instead of only using +/- or hotkeys, with a confirmation step before the change applies
  • Added an optional buzz/sound confirmation when a synced hotkey registers a kill — a per-grind toggle next to the grind status badge, plus a choice of five short sounds (Blip, Click, Ding, Heavy Click, Pop) with a live preview in Settings
  • Fixed a bug where old keyboard bindings from long-removed counters could still silently add to Diamond/Total Kills with nothing on screen to explain it
  • Settings → Grind Defaults now lets you set whether Click/Ding and Rare Fur tracking start on or off for new grinds (Rare Fur tracking now defaults on for new installs)
  • All Grinds Summary reorganized into a clean grid, and expanded with Avg kills per max-level, Total rares, Total diamonds, Total max-levels, and Total kills (all-time)
  • "Completed grinds" renamed to "Logged grinds"
  • Avg kills per diamond, Avg kills per max-level, Total rares, Total diamonds, Total max-levels, and Total kills now update live as you count kills on your current grind, instead of waiting until it's logged
  • Grind Log entries now show a Rares count alongside Diamonds/Max-Level/Total Kills for any grind that had rare tracking on
  • Added an "About" tab (under Settings/About) with information on the tool, its origin, and an FAQ
  • Renamed "How to Use Counter" to "How to Use Tool" and expanded it with fuller Log and Analytics sections
  • Removed Max-Weight Est tracking entirely — from the Advanced counter, All Grinds Summary, trend chart, Correlation, Grind Comparison, CSV export, and the Grind Log
  • Renamed the Advanced counter's "Max-Level" button to "Trolls" and simplified its cascading logic
  • Removed on-counter hint tooltips, now unnecessary with the simplified cascading
  • Removed the Correlation section entirely — Diamonds and Max-Level kills overlap by construction, which made the underlying math unreliable
  • Removed separate Basic/Advanced counter styles — there's now one unified counter (Diamond / Trolls / Total Kills), and all Great One grinds (past and future) now feed into All Grinds Summary stats, trend, and comparison
  • Added the ability to share a logged grind or your All Grinds overview as a branded image, with native share-sheet support and a download fallback
  • Share/Download Overview image now includes all the new All Grinds Summary stats, grouped into Grind Counts, Averages, and All-Time Totals sections
  • Replaced the placeholder app icon with the tool's new logo (hexagon + antler mark)
  • Moved backup/import controls out of the header into a new "Backup & Transfer Data" section under Settings
  • Importing a backup now compares your current data to the file and lets you choose Merge (recommended) or Overwrite, instead of overwriting automatically
  • Backups now also include keybinds, custom species/maps, and settings — not just grinds — for a full-parity transfer between browsers/devices
  • Added the tool's logo next to the title in the header
  • Reorganized the About/Feedback tab — FAQ now appears earlier, and "The Tool Has Four Parts" is renamed to "The Basic Idea"
  • Added a Ko-fi support button to the About/Feedback tab
  • Backup & Transfer Data buttons in Settings are now full-width and evenly spaced
  • Documented the share/download image feature and auto-save/sync behavior in the Log sections of the About and How to Use Tool tabs
  • Fixed a bug where starting a duplicate of an already-open grind could incorrectly mark an unrelated grind as logged and the new grind would never appear — the tool now simply tells you a duplicate already exists instead
  • Trend Across Grinds can now be filtered to one specific species, map, and platform combo to see just that instance's history over time
  • Trend chart bars now show Total Kills alongside Diamonds and Max-Level, with a hover tooltip on each bar showing its exact count and a hover tooltip on each entry showing the grind's name
  • Fixed the "Got it" button on info pop-ups (like the duplicate-grind warning) showing mismatched orange-on-orange text
  • Renamed "About/Feedback" to "About" and "FAQ" to "FAQ and Feedback"
  • Added a profile picture and YouTube channel link to the About tab, and rewrote the About text, "Who is this tool for?", and "How was this tool created?" in the creator's own words
  • Renamed the tool from "Great One Grind Counter" to "Great One Grind Log"
  • Added basic protection against the app being copied and reuploaded to another domain
  • Added a Terms of Service page, linked from the About tab, with a one-time agreement prompt on first use covering both the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  • Rewrote the Privacy Policy to accurately reflect what's actually collected — invite codes and feedback submissions only, no names or emails — and linked it from the About tab
  • Updated the non-affiliation disclaimer wording to match the official Fan Content Policy language from the game's developer
  • Added a direct contact email for questions about the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, and to the About tab
  • When creating a custom species, you're no longer asked for a max level — it's set automatically
  • Fixed the custom species/map creation windows to match the app's visual style
  • Added an Export & Import section to the How to Use Tool tab, and colored its section headings to match the rest of the app
  • Added step-by-step instructions for installing the tool as a browser app on Chrome/Edge, Android, iPhone/iPad, Mac, and Firefox, with quick links from the About and Settings tabs
  • Optimized the layout for phones and tablets — larger buttons, bigger text, and stacked layouts on smaller screens
  • First-time users now get an optional pop-up after agreeing to the Terms/Privacy prompt, pointing them to the How to Use Tool tab for the browser-app install guide, data export/import, and the basics of the counter
  • Hovering the Click/Ding and Rare Fur toggles on the counter now shows a hint pointing to the Settings tab for more options
  • Deleting a logged grind from the Grind Log now asks for one extra confirmation before the usual delete confirmation, since it removes real trophy data
  • Fixed a bug where deleting a grind from a filtered "Select Other (Open) Grind" list wouldn't actually delete it
  • Renamed "Buzz/Ding" to "Click/Ding" everywhere the hotkey feedback toggle is referenced
  • Editing a logged grind's counter now shows a Save Changes / Revert to Original confirmation, including a before/after breakdown of each counter, instead of just closing
  • Trying to switch tabs while a logged grind's counter has unsaved edits, or while a rename box is open, now asks you to finish first instead of silently discarding your changes
  • Fixed mobile pages rendering as a shrunk-down desktop layout — this was causing tiny text/buttons and dead space at the bottom of the screen on phones; phones now render at their actual width
  • Switched to a smarter caching strategy so the tool always loads the latest version automatically, instead of occasionally needing a fresh reinstall to see updates
  • Rare Fur counter note now explicitly states it doesn't affect Total Kills
  • On phone: hid the keyboard-sync controls (not applicable without a physical keyboard), and turned the Avg Kills/Diamond and Session Goal widgets into a compact floating display in the top-right corner
  • On phone: reorganized tab navigation into two rows to reduce clutter, and reordered the All Grinds Summary stat boxes for clearer grouping
  • On phone: tapping the +/- counter buttons now triggers the same Click/Ding feedback as hotkeys (previously hotkey-only), with the toggle label adjusted to say "+/- buttons" instead of "hotkey"
  • Fixed typing into a field on phone (e.g. renaming a grind) causing the page to zoom in and not zoom back out
  • On phone: reorganized the About tab's feedback/legal buttons into a two-row layout
  • Added a note under Feedback and Future Changes clarifying that feedback isn't guaranteed to be implemented
  • Fixed the iOS home-screen icon appearing with a large black border around a small logo instead of filling the icon properly
  • The Avg Kills/Diamond and Session Goal widgets can now be dragged and dropped anywhere on phone or laptop, snapping to whichever screen corner they're released nearest to; each remembers its own position
  • Increased the gap between the Avg Kills/Diamond and Session Goal widgets so they read as two separate cards instead of one connected block
  • Fixed the Information and Settings/About tab groups not actually sharing a row together on phone as intended
  • Fixed a jitter where tapping a +/- counter button would briefly shift the whole page down and back up
  • Removed the Notes field above Log Great One — it wasn't shown anywhere after saving, so it was more confusing than useful
  • Moved the auto-save explanation note to sit directly under the save status it describes, instead of the page footer
  • Fixed the beta invite-code screen briefly flashing on screen for a moment when reloading the app
  • Fixed the "Update available" notification not showing up reliably — it now checks periodically instead of only once per page load
  • Left-aligned the auto-save note under the "Saved" status (previously centered)
  • Improved layout scaling on smaller phone screens (e.g. iPhone SE) so buttons and text don't feel as cramped
  • Optimized the layout for large monitors and wide browser windows — wider content area and larger text/cards so the app doesn't look small on bigger screens
  • Renamed "Grind Comparison" to "Map Comparison"
  • Map Comparison now requires selecting a platform (PC/PlayStation/Xbox) in addition to species, so comparisons don't mix data across platforms
  • Fixed the Map Comparison chart not being centered in its viewing window
  • Added pinch-to-zoom for the Map Comparison chart on touch devices, alongside the existing +/- zoom buttons
  • Fixed the whole page scrolling along when dragging to pan the Map Comparison chart on phone — only the chart moves now
  • Changed the Trolls counter's icon from an antler to a cracked/broken diamond, to better reflect a max-level kill that didn't make diamond
  • Editing a logged grind's counter now always shows a before/after review of your changes before saving or reverting — including when switching tabs mid-edit or using Revert to Original — instead of a plain confirmation prompt
  • Noted in How to Use Tool that the app is currently optimized for phones, laptops, and PC monitors
  • Added a one-time pop-up right after the Terms/Privacy agreement offering to walk new users through installing the tool as a browser app, shown before the existing How to Use Tool guide pop-up
