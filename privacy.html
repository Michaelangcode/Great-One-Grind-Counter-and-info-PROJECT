What Data We Collect

The app currently stores your grind tracking data (kills, trophies, session progress) locally on your own device using browser storage. We do not currently collect or transmit this data to any server.

If future versions add account creation, we will collect an email address and basic usage data at that time, and this policy will be updated before that feature goes live. Any such collection would only happen with your permission — for example, by choosing to create an account — and never without your knowledge or consent.

Beta Version Invite Codes

A separate beta version of the app requires an invite code to access. If you enter a valid code, that code is submitted to a form we use to track which codes have been used. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information as part of this — just the code itself. This information is only submitted if you choose to enter an invite code; doing so constitutes your consent to this collection.

This data is processed using Google Forms. Google acts as our data processor for this purpose; their handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We store the results privately and do not share this data with any other third party.

Invite code data is retained for as long as we find it useful for tracking the beta program.

Feedback Form Data

If you use the in-app "Report a Bug / Give Feedback" link, you'll be taken to a form where you choose a feedback type (bug, feature request, or general feedback) and enter a message. We do not collect your email address or any other identifying information through this form — just the type and message content you provide. This information is only collected if you choose to submit the form; doing so constitutes your consent to this collection.

This data is processed using Google Forms and Google Sheets, governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We store the resulting Sheets privately and do not share this data with any other third party.

Feedback submissions are retained for as long as we find them useful for reviewing and improving the app.

Because neither the invite code nor feedback forms collect personal identifiers, we generally have no way to locate or delete a specific past submission on request. If you have a concern about something you submitted, contact us through the method listed below and we'll do what we reasonably can.

How Data Is Used

Locally stored data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality — tracking your grind progress between visits on the same device and browser.

How Data Is Stored

Data lives in your browser's local storage on your device. It is not currently backed up to any external server and will not sync across devices unless a future account system is introduced.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The app uses local device storage (not traditional tracking cookies) to remember your progress and preferences. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under the GDPR (EU users) or CCPA (California users) regarding access, correction, or deletion of any personal data collected. Since data is currently stored only on your device, you can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this app.

Oregon residents: Oregon does not currently have a comprehensive consumer privacy law in effect, so we apply GDPR/CCPA-level protections as our standard regardless of your location.

Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you clear it yourself or uninstall/reset the app. We do not currently hold a server-side copy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the feedback option in the app, or by emailing nyxhunting@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves, particularly if account creation or payment features are added. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date above.
