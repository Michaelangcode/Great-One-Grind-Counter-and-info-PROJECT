Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Great One Grind Counter ("the app", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

What Data We Collect

The app currently stores your grind tracking data (kills, trophies, session progress) locally on your own device using browser storage. We do not currently collect or transmit this data to any server.

If future versions add account creation, we will collect an email address and basic usage data at that time, and this policy will be updated before that feature goes live.

Beta Tester Program Data

If you participate in our closed beta testing program, we additionally collect your name, email address, unique invite code, and the date you accept our beta Non-Disclosure Agreement. This information is collected solely to manage invitations, verify NDA acceptance, and communicate with testers about the program.

If you submit feedback through our in-app feedback form, we collect the content of that submission along with your email address, used solely to follow up on or triage your feedback.

This data is processed using Google Forms and Google Sheets. Google acts as our data processor for this purpose; their handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We store the resulting Sheets privately and do not share this data with any other third party.

Beta program data is retained for the duration of the closed beta and for up to 90 days afterward, then deleted, unless you request earlier deletion.

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your beta tester data at any time by contacting us through the method listed below. We will act on deletion requests within a reasonable time.

Participation in the beta program, including this data collection, is voluntary and requires your consent via invite code entry and NDA acceptance.

How Data Is Used

Locally stored data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality — tracking your grind progress between visits on the same device and browser.

How Data Is Stored

Data lives in your browser's local storage on your device. It is not currently backed up to any external server and will not sync across devices unless a future account system is introduced.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The app uses local device storage (not traditional tracking cookies) to remember your progress and preferences. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under the GDPR (EU users) or CCPA (California users) regarding access, correction, or deletion of any personal data collected. Since data is currently stored only on your device, you can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this app.

Oregon residents: Oregon does not currently have a comprehensive consumer privacy law in effect, so we apply GDPR/CCPA-level protections as our standard regardless of your location.

Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you clear it yourself or uninstall/reset the app. We do not currently hold a server-side copy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the feedback option in the app, or to the contact method listed on our main site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves, particularly if account creation or payment features are added. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date above.
